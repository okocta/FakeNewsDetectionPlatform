About Us

Welcome to the Fake News Detection Platform, a cutting-edge tool designed to empower individuals, researchers, and journalists to evaluate the credibility of news articles. Our mission is to promote media literacy and combat the spread of misinformation.

Through our automated news verification system and community-driven discussions, we aim to create a transparent and reliable space for news analysis and engagement.

Together, we can foster informed decision-making and build a more trustworthy media ecosystem.
